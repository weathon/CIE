Coastal International Education

About

Coastal International Education is an organization dedicated to improving the English communication ability for everyone who requires it. “A-B-C” policy is what C.I.E. pursues. It stands for Active learning, Broadened Horizon, and Consistent Interest. The focus for Coastal International Education is to increase the interest in learning English and the communication ability of students. Learning passively is a problem for the majority of students, and the reason for it is that the content they have been taught is not attractive enough. With our 1v1 teaching style, our teachers can have a deeper understanding of that one specific student and know what his/her interest is. Therefore, it can make the class more attractive to the student. At Coastal International Education, the teacher is not only a teacher, they are also the friend, the psychologist, and the adviser for the success of that student’s future.
